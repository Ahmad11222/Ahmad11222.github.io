Privacy Policy

When you access or use the services, or otherwise interact with us, we may collect certain categories of personal information about you from a variety of sources.

Categories of personal information that we may collect directly from individuals include:

  • Basic personal Information. We collect tenants’ names so that they can appear on the home page.
  • Contact Information. We can address them if there is a need.
  • Communications. We collect communications that individuals share with us in connection with customer support requests, questions submitted through our website, and requests for product information.
  • Identity verification information. When you schedule a self-guided tour at a partner property, we will collect a scan of your driver’s license in order to verify your identity.

How we use personal information:

  • Provide the Service
  • Communication
  • Comply with the law and exercise our rights.
  • Corporate transactions

How and When We Disclose Your Personal Information:

We do not sell personal information to anyone for any purpose. We may disclose your personal information to the following categories of recipients, to be used only for legitimate purposes in keeping with this Privacy Notice:

  • Group companies and affiliates. We may disclose, share, or transfer your information to any business entity that is part of our corporate family.
  • Service providers and professional advisors. We may disclose your information to third party vendors, service providers and contractors who provide services in support of our business operations. This can include landlords, property owners, and our advertising and analytics providers. We may also disclose your personal information to our professional advisors such as our attorneys, accountants, and insurance providers. In addition, when you sign up for a self-guided tour of a property, we collect your government-issued ID (i.e., your driver’s license) and a photo “selfie” that you take of yourself and we provide them to our vendor, which compares information from both documents to verify your identity in order to enable us to prevent theft or other security incidents in connection with the Services. Our vendor may collect and store biometric information in order to provide us with this service, but we do not receive or store or otherwise use any biometric information. We instruct our vendor to comply with all applicable privacy laws, including those governing biometric information.
  • Parties involved in a corporate transaction. We may disclose your information to relevant third parties in the event of a divestiture, merger, consolidation, or asset sale, or in the unlikely event of bankruptcy.
  • Law enforcement or other governmental entities. We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or if we believe in good faith that such action is necessary to comply with the law, prevent unlawful activity, defend our rights, or maintain security.
  • Other third parties with your consent. We may disclose your information to any other third party where you have provided consent to such disclosure.
